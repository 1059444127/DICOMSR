Cardiolizer
-----------
	The program itself.
	It stores the procedures to compound the reports.

CrapFixer
---------
	It stores the procedures to fix external system problems.
	It tries to adapt Cardiolizer to the clinic functioning without changing the environment.

StructuredReport
----------------
	Where the dictionaries are.
	Here I store the IDs and Path to get info from de StructuredReports made by the clients.

Utilities
---------
	Where extensions to the .NET Framework are.
	Here I store the procedures to get work like compressing or moving files done.

VisualTools
-----------
	Little GUI applications that help me to test and understand how some things work.
